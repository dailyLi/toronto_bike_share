The monthly ridership plot revealed the impact of weather: summer definitely is the season when more people use Share Bike Toronto. From the ridership plot by each day of 2021, we found a few outstanding "spikes" on: 05/16, 05/24, 06/05, 06/12, 07/10. All these days seemed to be gamedays (baseball and basketball), even though the baseball game didn't return to Rogers Centre until Jul 30.
